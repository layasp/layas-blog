MY EXPERIENCE AT SPEAK FOR INDIA TAMILNADU EDITION

Speak for India is an initiative by Federal Bank in association with The Hindustan Times Group [ltd] to encourage young, dynamic and enthusiastic minds across India. I participated in the Coimbatore district level competition conducted under the TamilNadu edition 23-24. In the first round, I competed against 120 130 other competitors from various colleges across Coimbatore. In this round, we were given an on-the-spot topic, on which we had to speak for 1 minute. The top 30 students were selected to proceed to the next round. I was elated to have proceeded to the next round; here the participants were grouped into teams of 6 and we had to speak for and against the given topic for 2 minutes and 15 seconds each. I was selected among the top 16 to proceed to the ZONAL LEVEL[TN EDITION].


ROUND 1

  1. TOPIC : Offline education is better than online education
  2. TIME: 1 min
  3. STANCE: For
ROUND 2
